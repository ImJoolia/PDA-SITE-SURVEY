Tell us about your last visit to SITE.

This summer, SITE SANTA FE is excited to welcome you (yes, you!) to a series of public design workshops, co-created by our friends at Public Design Agency, as part of a participatory planning and visioning project to shape the future of visitor experience at SITE SANTA FE.

Hosted by Public Design Agency and students from the UNM School of Architecture and Planning (UNM SA+P), “Summer of Participatory Planning + Visioning” is an invitation to Santa Fe residents and visitors to provide feedback, insight, and design solutions on how to make visiting SITE SANTA FE more accessible, family-friendly, equitable, and enjoyable.

Thank you for completing this brief survey and for becoming a co-designer with us!
